Connect & disconnect

Jack · Last updated July 21, 2026

Connecting

The owner connects a QuickBooks Online company by authorizing it through Intuit's standard OAuth consent screen. Access is granted per company and is read-only.

Disconnecting / revoking access

Access can be revoked at any time, either side:

  1. From Intuit (recommended): sign in to QuickBooks Online → Settings ⚙ → Apps (or account settings → Connected apps) → find this app → Disconnect. This immediately revokes the app's tokens for that company.
  2. From the owner's computer: remove the local connector and its stored token, e.g. claude mcp remove qbo-<company> and delete the company's local token file. This removes the app's ability to reconnect.

Because the app stores nothing on any external server, revoking access and deleting the local token fully disconnects it.
