Jack

A private, internal business assistant — run locally by its owner.

Jack is a personal tool the owner runs on their own computer to help manage their own small businesses. With the owner's permission, Jack can read the owner's own QuickBooks Online accounting data to answer questions like “what's our cash position?” or “show this month's P&L.”

What it is

  • Private & internal. Used only by the owner, for the owner's own companies. Not sold, listed, or offered to other users.
  • Read + confirmed edits. Jack reads accounting data to report on it, and can create/update records only after the owner confirms each specific change. It never moves money (no payments/transfers/bill payments/deposits/refunds) and never deletes records.
  • Local. It runs on the owner's own machine. Authorization tokens are stored locally on that machine.

Connecting & disconnecting QuickBooks

Access is granted by the owner through Intuit's standard OAuth flow and can be revoked at any time. See the Disconnect page.
